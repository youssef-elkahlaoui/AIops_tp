# ENSA Al Hoceima - Academic Programs

## Engineering Cycles

### Cycle Préparatoire Intégré (CPI)

- **Duration:** 2 years
- **Admission:** Baccalauréat scientifique with competitive selection
- **Subjects:** Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, Languages

### Cycle Ingénieur

- **Duration:** 3 years
- **Specializations available:**
  - Génie Informatique (Software Engineering)
  - Génie Civil (Civil Engineering)
  - Génie Électrique (Electrical Engineering)

## Génie Informatique (Computer Science Engineering)

### Core Courses

- Algorithms and Data Structures
- Database Systems
- Software Engineering
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Web and Mobile Development
- Network and Security
- Cloud Computing

### Technologies Taught

- Programming: Python, Java, C++, JavaScript
- Frameworks: React, Angular, Spring Boot, Django
- Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB
- DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD
- Cloud: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud

## Génie Civil (Civil Engineering)

### Core Courses

- Structural Analysis
- Construction Materials
- Geotechnical Engineering
- Hydraulics
- Project Management
- Building Information Modeling (BIM)

## Student Projects

Students complete several projects during their studies:

1. **PFA (Projet de Fin d'Année):** End of year project
2. **PFE (Projet de Fin d'Études):** Final graduation project with industry partners
3. **Internships:** Mandatory internships in companies

## Research Labs

ENSA Al Hoceima hosts several research laboratories:

- Laboratory of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence
- Laboratory of Civil Engineering and Environment
- Laboratory of Renewable Energy

## International Partnerships

ENSA Al Hoceima has partnerships with:

- Universities in France, Spain, and Germany
- Exchange programs with European institutions
- Joint research projects with international labs
