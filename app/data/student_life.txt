ENSA Al Hoceima - Student Life Guide
=====================================

LOCATION AND CAMPUS
-------------------
ENSA Al Hoceima is located in the beautiful coastal city of Al Hoceima in northern Morocco.
Address: BP 03, Ajdir, Al Hoceima 32003, Morocco
The campus offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and the Rif Mountains.

ACADEMIC CALENDAR
-----------------
- Fall Semester: September to January
- Spring Semester: February to June
- Exams: January and June
- Summer Break: July and August

STUDENT CLUBS AND ACTIVITIES
----------------------------
1. Club Informatique (Computer Club)
   - Hackathons and coding competitions
   - Tech workshops and seminars
   - Open source projects

2. Club Robotique (Robotics Club)
   - Robot building competitions
   - Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects
   - National robotics championships

3. Club Culturel (Cultural Club)
   - Art exhibitions
   - Music and theater performances
   - Cultural exchange events

4. Club Sportif (Sports Club)
   - Football, basketball, volleyball teams
   - Annual sports tournament
   - Beach activities

5. Club Entrepreneuriat (Entrepreneurship Club)
   - Startup incubation
   - Business plan competitions
   - Networking events with professionals

TIPS FOR NEW STUDENTS
---------------------
1. Join at least one club to meet other students
2. Visit the library early to get familiar with resources
3. Attend orientation week activities
4. Connect with senior students for guidance
5. Explore Al Hoceima city and its beautiful beaches
6. Learn some Amazigh (Berber) phrases - locals appreciate it!

Al Hoceima is known for its beautiful Quemado Beach and the nearby Al Hoceima National Park.
The city offers a peaceful environment perfect for studying while enjoying Mediterranean culture.
